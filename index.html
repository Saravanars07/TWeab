Our Tea Philosophy

Ancient Wisdom in Every Sip

Born from the misty hills of Darjeeling and the bustling chai stalls of Mumbai, Chai Nirvana represents the perfect harmony of tradition and innovation. Our master blender, with 30 years of experience, personally selects each ingredient.

We don't just serve tea - we curate experiences. From the temperature of the water to the precise steeping time, every detail is perfected to unlock the full potential of our premium tea leaves and spices.
